## XAUUSD — Macro-Sensitive · Sweep-Heavy · Volatile in LO/NYO

### Traits

- Macro-sensitive (yields, USD, risk sentiment)
- Frequent sweeps
- Largest volatility spikes at LO and NYO

### Volatility Signature

- Expansion behaviour common
- Slippage risk in fast moves
- OB/FVG precision degrades in high volatility

### Macro Context (Non-Directional)

- Rising DXY → bearish pressure
- Rising yields → bearish pressure
- Rising VIX → flight-to-safety support
- SPX falling → potential bullish tone

### Session Behaviour

- **Asian**: mean reversion, VWAP scalps
- **LO**: sweep → expansion common
- **London**: clearer continuation or trap reversal
- **NYO**: strongest volatility
- **NY late**: only MR, avoid continuations

### Strategy DNA

- MR, VWAP
- Sweep → CHOCH → retrace
- Trap reversals

### Invalid Conditions

- Scalping during volatility expansion
- Breakouts without volume
- Tight SL (<20–30 pips)
- Mid-range entries

### SL/TP

- SL: 20–40
- TP: 40–120 depending on volatility
