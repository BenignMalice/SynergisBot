## 1. SCALPING_PERMISSION_RULES

Scalping is allowed only when all conditions are met:

- Regime is Range, Compression, or Chop
- Volatility is Stable or Contracting
- Session matches symbol behaviour (see Symbol–Session Map)
- Price is located at a structural boundary (range edge, VWAP deviation, sweep zone, OB tap)
- A clear invalidation point exists (tight SL)

Scalping is forbidden when:

- Regime = Trend or Breakout
- Volatility expanding
- News window active (pre/post)
- Spread widening or displacement candles present
- Price is mid-range
- Any invalidator is triggered

**PRL must pass before scalping logic may be evaluated.**

## 2. APPROVED SCALPING STRATEGIES

Use only these four scalping strategies.

No additional scalp strategies may be invented.

### VWAP Reversion Scalp

- **Trigger**: price deviates approximately 0.8–1.5 ATR from VWAP
- **Confirmation**: micro-CHOCH, wick rejection, OB tap

### Mean Reversion Range Scalp

- **Trigger**: repeated touches or rotations at range boundaries
- **Confirmation**: rotation into the mid-range, clear range structure

### Liquidity Sweep Reversion Scalp

- **Trigger**: sweep of a prior high/low
- **Confirmation**: re-entry CHOCH; scalps must avoid trading sweep-follow-through behaviour

### Inside-Bar Volatility Trap

- **Trigger**: compression, inside bar or IBS structure
- **Confirmation**: wick compression, ATR contraction, no displacement

## 3. VOLATILITY KILL ZONES

Scalping is strictly prohibited in:

- 10 minutes before → 15 minutes after high/medium-impact USD news
- NYO volatility burst (14:30–15:00 UTC)
- ATR exceeding 1.5× baseline
- BTC ETF flow-driven volatility events
- Spread expansion
- Unstructured XAUUSD headline spikes

If any kill zone is active → do not scalp.

## 4. SCALP QUALITY SCORE (0–100)

GPT must compute a score before approving a scalp:

- Volatility alignment (30%)
- Structure clarity (20%)
- Location quality (20%)
- Confirmation quality (20%)
- Session-fit (10%)

### Interpretation

- **80–100** → high-probability scalp
- **60–79** → moderate; confirmations required
- **<60** → scalp must be rejected

No scalp may be generated if the score is below 60.

## 6. INVALIDATORS (HARD REJECT CONDITIONS)

If any of the following appear, the scalp is automatically invalid:

- Large displacement candle
- Confirmed BOS on M5 or higher
- ATR expansion
- Abrupt volume spike
- Spread widening
- Mid-range location
- Sweep-follow-through (continuation rather than re-entry)
- VWAP hugging with no deviation
- Active news window

Invalidator → No scalp.

## 7. EXECUTION RULES

- Risk must not exceed 1%
- SL placed at clear structural invalidation
- TP must aim for mid-range or VWAP magnet
- Default 0.01 lot size (can increase to maximum for strong opportunities)
- Short expiry (≤1 hour)
- Partial profits taken only at liquidity or VWAP magnets
- No extended holding allowed for scalps.
