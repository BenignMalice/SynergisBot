# **XAUUSD (Gold)**

## Behaviour Characteristics
- Highly reactive to USD, yields, inflation data  
- Extreme liquidity sweeps around London Open and NYO  
- Deep pullbacks → high trap frequency  
- Very sensitive to VIX changes  

## Volatility Signature
- ADR: **150–350 pips**  
- Fast expansion waves; slippage risk high  
- London & NYO = explosive moves  
- Asian = mean reversion, compression  

## Macro Bias Multipliers
- DXY ↑ → **strong bearish multiplier**  
- US10Y ↑ → **bearish**  
- VIX ↑ → **bullish (risk-off)** but only if USD not strengthening  
- S&P500 ↓ → **bullish**  

## Session Behaviour
| Session | Behaviour | Best Play |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Asian | Low volatility, tight ranges | VWAP/MR scalps |
| London Open | Sweep → expansion common | Reversal traps |
| London | Expansion, trend building | Continuation |
| NY Open | Highest volatility | Breakouts |
| NY Late | Reversion | Avoid trend trades |

## Strategy DNA
- Best: liquidity sweeps, MR, VWAP fades, fakeout reversals  
- Breakouts only with volume  
- Avoid trend scalps in Asian  

## Invalid Conditions
- Do NOT scalp during expanding volatility  
- Avoid tight SL (< 20–30 pips)  
- Avoid breakouts without volume  

## SL/TP Behaviour
- SL: **20–40 pips** for scalps  
- TP: **40–120 pips**  
- Wide SL required during volatility expansions

# **BTCUSD (Bitcoin)**

## Behaviour Characteristics
- Deep retracements even in strong trends  
- London = fakeout → sweep → expansion  
- NY = continuation engine  
- Likes to hunt both sides before moving  

## Volatility Signature
- ADR: **800–3000 points**  
- Volatility clustering common  
- NY session dominates trend days  

## Macro Bias Multipliers
- NAS100 ↑ → bullish  
- VIX ↑ → bearish BTC (risk-off)  
- USD strength → bearish BTC  
- Liquidity conditions matter more than SMC structure  

## Session Behaviour
| Session | Behaviour | Best Play |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Asian | Accumulation | OB scalps |
| London | Fakeout heavy | Sweep → reversal |
| NYO | Momentum | Breakouts/continuation |
| NY Late | Volatile | Quick scalps only |

## Strategy DNA
- Best: sweep→CHOCH reversals, deep pullback continuations  
- Avoid: tight-range MR during expanding volatility  

## Invalid Conditions
- Do NOT use tight SL (< 40–80 points)  
- Avoid breakouts without volume  
- Avoid fading NY continuation unless sweep confirmed  

## SL/TP Behaviour
- SL: **80–200+ points**  
- TP1 early (BTC retraces often)

# **EURUSD**

## Behaviour Characteristics
- Most technical & stable FX pair  
- London defines direction  
- NY session follows USD strength  

## Volatility Signature
- ADR: **40–80 pips**  
- Very clean levels, very structured  

## Macro Bias Multipliers
- DXY ↑ → bearish EURUSD  
- US yields ↑ → bearish  
- Risk-on → bullish  

## Session Behaviour
| Session | Behaviour | Best Play |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Asian | Tight | MR scalps |
| London | Trend clarity | Breakouts + continuation |
| NY | USD-driven | Continuation or reversal |

## Strategy DNA
- Best: trend continuation, structured breakouts  
- MR only when inside established range  

## Invalid Conditions
- Avoid trading without session alignment  
- Avoid fading trends too early  

## SL/TP Behaviour
- SL: **10–20 pips**  
- TP follows ADR availability

# **GBPUSD**

## Behaviour Characteristics
- Volatile, spike-prone  
- London Open highly manipulative  
- NY continuation reliable  

## Volatility Signature

- Large wicks, trap patterns common  

## Macro Bias Multipliers
- DXY strength → heavy bearish GBP  
- UK news → high volatility  
- Correlates heavily with risk appetite  

## Session Behaviour
| Session | Behaviour | Best Play |
|--------|-----------|-----------|
| Asian | Fairly quiet | MR |
| London Open | Trap zone | Sweeps |
| London | Volatile | Only enter after confirmation |
| NY | Expansion | Continuation |

## Strategy DNA
- Best: fakeout → reversal, sweep traps  
- Breakouts valid only with volatility support  

## Invalid Conditions
- Avoid early London continuation entries  
- Avoid tight SL (< 15–20 pips)
